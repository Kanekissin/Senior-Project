Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight is a side-scrolling RPG that emersises you in the quest of The Knight. You fight bosses that will amaze and terrify you at the same time. Team Cherry developed this game
extremely well and used a beautiful art style as well as creating a phenomenal sound track. If you've ever wanted to wander an underground bug kingdom and kill release your anger on some bugs,
this game is for you. This game is often compared to Dark Souls yet it has a completely different astetic and story that works extremely well with the underlaying trap of bosses that are extremely hard.
