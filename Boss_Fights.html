All Boss Fights In Hollow Knight

Absolute Radiance

The total end game boss. The only tip I have for her is patience and repition is key.

Brooding Mawlek

No clue how to describe this boss other then a cool mechanical looking bug that shoots infection.
Wait until you get the "Mothwing Cloak" at the earliest to fight. Dash in, hit, dash out. Constantly keep moving

Broken Vessel

This boss esentially has the same moveset at you. Do not try to out do the boss in speed nor should you try to tank it's hits. Just stay moving constantly and stay just out of range.

Collector

This boss is pretty weird but is rather easy. Keep moving and stay behind The collector whenever possible. Most enemies that come out of the glass bowls will be taken out in one hit.

Crystal Guardian

Astetically speaking an amazing boss. stay airborn and avoid lazers. It seems like it should be harder then that but it's not

Dung Defender

A really crappy boss if I do say so. When he bounces around in a ball form you can juggle him and you can use "Desolate Dive" to draw him out when he's underground

Elder Hu

A rather easy Dream warrior. You can almost always get to one of the gaps in his attacks either just by walking there or by dashing.

Enraged Guardian

Literally the Crystal Guardian but faster.

Failed Champion

Don't stay underneth. Whenever he goes to swing his hammer, hit then dash away.

False Knight

Same as the Failed Champion except slower and less health

Flukemarm

This is a weird ugly worm. Just use Howling Wraiths or Abyssal Shriek to tear her to shreads.

Galien

I don't really have any tips for this Dream Warrior, he's not a hard fight, just weird in being that you can usually tank through his hits or you can be strategic. It doens't really matter here.

God Tamer

Kill the beast before the Tamer and the Tamer will surrender. This can be done easily with Sharp Shadow

Gorb

Avoiding getting hit isn't hard and you can usually get in a few hits at a time on this Dream Warrior

Great Nailsage Sly

Patience is key, as well as persistance.

Grey Prince Zote

Hit then dodge and repeat

Grimm

Arguably my favorite fight right after the Mantis Lords/Sisters of battle fights, just learn the auditory and visual ques.

Gruz Mother

Dodge a lot, and hit inbetween dodging

Hive Knight

Sharp Shadow through him

Hollow Knight

Slow and steady wins the race. Or kills your brother ig...
Either way just slowly chip away at his health however you find you can do it.

Hornet

Pogo her as much as possible, do not attack from underneth nor the sides

Hunter

Not an actual boss but is rather a trick boss

Lost Kin

He has the same moveset as you, gain as much soul as possible and use ranged spells

Mantis Lords

Memorize the attacks and account for it

Markoth

Markoth sucks, hes annoying and you just gotta keep on going back and forth until you win

Marmu

Juggle this Dream warrior like your life depends on it

Massive Moss Charger

Keep htting it back again and again

Nailmasters Oro and Mato

This is a fun fight, when Oro isn't attacking Mato is and vice versa.
Simply get a hit in whenever you can safely do and focus on dodging

Nightmare King Grimm

2nd hardest boss, next to Absolute Radiance. Patience and persistance is key

No Eyes

This is my favorite Dream Warrior.
Either pogo or hit her from underneth with a dash ready just in case

Nosk

Cheese him by the bottom left part of the elevated platform

Oblobbles

Damage both evenly

Paintmaster Sheo

Memorize the patterns and simple counters

Pure Vessel

Fast paced throw hands extra hard extra fast

Radiance

Rinse and Repeat

Sisters of Battle

See Mantis Lords

Soul Master

Memorize the patterns, get a hit in whenever you can

Soul Tyrant

Same as Soul Master

Soul Warrior

Dodge and hit, Rinse and Repeat

Tiso

Fake Fight

Traitor Lord

Dash through and dodge

Uumuu

Wait for Quaril to attack.
If in GODHOME, tank the damage or use shade soul to push the things into it

Vengefly King

Pogo for days

Watcher Knight

Chandelier above kills one then sharp shadow

White Defender

Same as dung defender

Winged Nosk

Nosk, but flying

Xero

Keep moving

Zote

Smack him around a bit
